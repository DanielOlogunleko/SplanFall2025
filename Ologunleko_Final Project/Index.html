The Four Mythological Creatures of China

The Four Symbols are legendary guardians tied to ancient Chinese astronomy. The sky was organized into four regions, each containing seven lunar mansions (together: the 28 Mansions). These symbols helped track seasons and the movement of the Moon across the heavens.

Each quadrant is represented by a mythic protector: the Azure Dragon of the East, the Vermilion Bird of the South, the White Tiger of the West, and the Black Tortoise of the North. Beyond astronomy, they represent balance—direction, season, and elemental forces.
